Hippo Steak Burger


A real treasure.

This burger is one of the best that I have ever had, and here's how to make it.

Ingredient Amount
Raw Meat 6 oz
Black Pepper 1 Tsp
Cumin 1/2 Tsp
Lettuce 1 Leaf
Cheddar Cheese 1 Slice
Ketchup 1 Tbsp
Sesame Bun 1 Bun

Shape the meat with a burger press, and cook for 7-8 minutes, or until pink is almost gone. Put the elements together, and enjoy!
