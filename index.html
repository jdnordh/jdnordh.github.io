About

I’m a software developer who enjoys turning complex ideas into clean, reliable systems.

My background is primarily in backend development, working with .NET and modern application architecture. Over time, I’ve naturally moved toward a more product-focused role—collaborating closely with stakeholders, shaping requirements, and helping teams ship better software, faster.

I care a lot about how things are built. Not just that they work—but that they’re maintainable, testable, and make sense to the next person who reads the code.

Lately, I’ve been especially interested in how AI can be integrated into real-world products in a practical way—not just as a feature, but as a way to rethink how software gets built.

Outside of coding, I’m usually playing hockey or thinking about how to improve systems—technical or otherwise.
